Wonderful Pancake Recipe

This quick and simple will have you having these pancakes every morning as it is the simplest pancake recipe on the web with a flavor that will melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • pancake mix
  • 3/4 cup milk(almond or regular)
  • eggs or egg whites
  • (optional)choclate chips
  • (optional) blueberries

    Directions
  1. Pour 1 cup of pancake mix into bowl or measuring cup
  2. Add milk into the bowl and stir
  3. Add the egg or egg whites into bowl and stir
  4. (optional) Ingredients above
  5. Use frying pan and flip when you see one side bubble
  6. ENJOY!!!
