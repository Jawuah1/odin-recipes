Tuna sandwich

Best Tuna sandwich recipe

This quick and simple will have you having craving tuna sandwiches every day as it is the simplest and best tasting tuna sandwich recipe on the web with a flavor that will melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choosing
  • Can of tuna
  • Pinche of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • tablespoon of Mayo
  • Cut up lettuce and tomatoes

    Directions
  1. Pour Tuna into a bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper into the bowl and stir
  3. Add mayo into bowl and stir
  4. Spread tuna mix on Bread
  5. Add lettuce and tomatoes
  6. ENJOY!!!
