Pepperoni Pizza

The outstanding Pepperoni Pizza

This will be a quick and easy recipe to make at home for you to eat and with these instruction it will have you wanting more of this outstanding Pepperoni Pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • mozzerella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • pre made pizza dough
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of basil
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder

    Directions
  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees for 5 minutes
  2. For sauce combine all the spices into the tomato sauce
  3. Spread the sauce on the pre made pizza dough
  4. Add in the cheese and then add in Pepperoni
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until crust is brown and until the cheese is nice and metled
  6. ENJOY!!!
